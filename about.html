My name is Kenya Mendez, and I'm currently a senior at Magic City Acceptance Academy. I was born in Guatemala, but when I was 11, my mother decided it would be better for us to come to the United States for a better life. For years, I've heard teachers ask students what major they were going to choose and which university they were going to attend. I knew I couldn't go to university because of financial constraints, but I could choose a more affordable major that I would enjoy. So, I decided to study cosmetology!

Cosmetology is the professional study and application of beauty treatments, including hair styling, skin care, cosmetics, and manicures/pedicures.I chose this career because I feel good about my appearance, and I love seeing people feel confident. I want to help all those people who are looking for a change or want to fix something they think is impossible and make it possible.
