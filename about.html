My name is Kenya Mendez, and I'm currently a senior at Magic City Acceptance Academy. I was born in Guatemala, but when I was 11, my mother decided that the best thing for us would be to come to the United States in search of a better life. For years, I've heard teachers ask students what major they were going to choose and which college they were going to attend. I knew I couldn't go to college due to financial limitations, but I could choose a more affordable major that I enjoyed. So, I decided to study cosmetology! Cosmetology is the professional study and application of beauty treatments, including hairstyling, skincare, cosmetics, and manicures/pedicures. I chose this major because I feel good about my appearance, and I love seeing people feel confident. I want to help all those people who are looking for a change or who want to solve something they think is impossible and make it possible.
