Responsible Internet Usage

Data Privacy

Be careful about which companies or individuals you share your personal information with—such as names, passwords, numbers, and anything else related to you.

Ethics

Do not disseminate false information; instead, engage proactively and thoughtfully rather than sharing impulsively.

Digital Citizenship

Respecting others in social media or respect in general, involves accepting people for who they are, validating their feelings, and valuing their perspectives, even during disagreements. It builds trust, safety, and healthy relationships through active listening, kindness, and consideration of personal space.

Industry Ethics

Designing technology with good intentions requires ethical approach that prioritizes user well-being, sustainability, and equity. This involves embedding empathy, transparency, and accountability into the design process, ensuring technology solves real problems without creating harmful side effects.
